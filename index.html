What is SafeCool?

SafeCool is a small device that attaches to your AC system, and notifies you when there's a problem.

The Device

SafeCool is a small device that attaches to the float switch on your air handler.

Connectivity

SafeCool periodically monitors the status of the float switch and records the history.

Real Time Alerts

If the drain line is backed up, SafeCool can sense there's a problem and automatically sends an alert.
